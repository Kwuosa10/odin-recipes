Chicken Stir-Fry with Vegetables

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts (sliced into thin strips)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey or brown sugar
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil (for stir-frying)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 zucchini (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (julienned)
  • 2-3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1-inch ginger (grated)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce (for stir-fry)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Prep the chicken: In a bowl, mix the chicken strips with 1 tbsp soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, and cornstarch. Set aside for about 10-15 minutes.
  2. Cook the chicken: Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chicken strips and stir-fry until golden and cooked through (about 5-6 minutes). Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Stir-fry the veggies: In the same skillet, add a little more oil if needed. Stir-fry the garlic, ginger, bell pepper, zucchini, and carrot for about 4-5 minutes until they are tender but still crisp.
  4. Combine: Return the chicken to the pan with the vegetables. Add 2 tbsp soy sauce and sesame oil. Toss everything together until well-coated and heated through.
  5. Serve: Serve over steamed rice or noodles for a complete meal.
